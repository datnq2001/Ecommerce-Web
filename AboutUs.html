About Us

About Us

Established in 1968 in the renowned tea plateau of Bao Loc (Lam Dong), Phuc Long aspires to bring high-quality tea and coffee products, preserving the traditional Vietnamese flavors combined with modern consumption styles.

In the 1980s, Phuc Long successively opened 3 stores on Le Van Sy, Tran Hung Dao, and Mac Thi Buoi streets to introduce authentic Vietnamese tea and coffee flavors to both domestic and international customers. Striving for continuous quality improvement, intellectual investment, and emotional commitment to ensure that each product aims to bond emotional connections, spreading joy to every customer with utmost appreciation, in 2000, Phuc Long Trading Manufacturing Company Limited was officially established.

In 2007, Phuc Long acquired a tea hill in Thai Nguyen and decided to invest in a tea & coffee processing factory in Binh Duong with the aim of ensuring the supply of quality products, serving the domestic market, and meeting export demand.

From the desire to deliver fresh and flavorful beverages directly brewed and served to customers in a modern style and reasonable price, the first Phuc Long Coffee & Tea store was launched at Crescent Mall Shopping Mall, District 7. This event marks a turning point in Phuc Long's development history, gradually becoming a rare Vietnamese brand officially participating in the Food & Beverage sector with self-service stores in modern, classy, and prominent spaces.

With over 50 years of nurturing excellence from green tea buds and high-grade coffee beans, along with the desire to offer customers the most valuable experiences when enjoying, Phuc Long continuously pioneers with many creative ideas leading the tea and coffee industry.

We believe that each tea and coffee product will be even more excellent when created from relentless efforts and passion. And it is the connection based on trust, honesty, and love that contributes to the beauty of tea & coffee culture soaring higher and farther. Continuing our efforts, from the first store to now, Phuc Long has built more than 60 stores in Ho Chi Minh City, Binh Duong, Bien Hoa, Can Tho, Da Nang, Nha Trang, and Hanoi areas to serve fresh drinks from tea and coffee. Not stopping there, we continue to expand the store system from South to North, increasing product coverage to all systems: supermarkets, convenience stores, etc.

Operating with the motto "Quality stems from passion," together, we are writing the story of a traditional Vietnamese brand, connecting the essence of many decades of experience and constantly refining, renewing ourselves in the passage of time to become a brand that is always attached to many generations of customers.

Vision

1. With an incessant desire to expand the market - sustainably develop, Phuc Long strives to become a company specializing in producing and exporting high-value tea and coffee in Vietnam.

2. Phuc Long aims to create a Vietnamese brand with class and quality demonstrated in each product, thereby gradually affirming its position in the international market.

Mission

1. Pioneering the era with innovative ideas leading the tea and coffee industry.

2. Together shining the brand, motivating each other, transforming ideas into reality with a spirit of connection, interaction, listening, and respect.

3. To create joy and emotion for customers, each of us must constantly shine. Do not let our own abilities fall asleep, constantly challenge difficulties, always innovate ourselves.

4. Create high value through understanding, empathy, and truly valuable proposals, satisfying customer satisfaction.

Core Values

1. For customers: Commitment to providing the best quality products and services.

2. For employees: Building a professional, dynamic, creative, and humane working environment, providing high income and equal development opportunities.

3. For society: Harmonizing corporate interests with social interests, actively contributing to community-oriented activities, demonstrating a sense of responsibility to society.

Operating Fields

1. Manufacturing, exporting, trading finished tea and coffee products (packaged)

2. Service business in-store dining system:

  • Various food and beverage items
  • Various cakes, snacks
  • Home delivery service
