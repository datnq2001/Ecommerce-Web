FROM TEA SEEDLINGS, WE CULTIVATE PASSION

With over 50 years of refining the essence from the finest green tea buds and premium coffee beans, alongside the desire to provide customers with the most valuable experiences, Phuc Long has continuously been a pioneering brand with many innovative ideas leading the tea and coffee industry.

We believe that each tea and coffee product will become even more exquisite when created with relentless effort and passion. And it is the connection based on trust, honesty, and love that will contribute to bringing out the beauty in the tea and coffee appreciation culture soaring higher and further.
