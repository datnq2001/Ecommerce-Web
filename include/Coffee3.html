CHERRY COFFEE

Cherry coffee, also known as Mit coffee, has beautiful shiny yellow beans. Although its flavor is not as robust as Robusta or as intense as Arabica, Cherry coffee has its own distinctive characteristics: slightly sour, slightly bitter, low caffeine content, and a faint aroma.

MOKA COFFEE

Moka coffee is a variety of coffee that is difficult to grow, belonging to the Arabica genus, commonly grown in Dalat, Lam Dong, especially at Cau Dat Moka. In Vietnam, Moka coffee is considered a truly rare product due to its excellent quality and exquisite taste: elegant fragrance, refreshing acidity.
