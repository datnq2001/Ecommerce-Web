OOLONG TEA

Oolong Tea is known as one of the great teas with delicate flavors. The processing of Oolong tea is very unique with a natural fermentation process. After harvesting, according to the principle of one bud and two leaves, the tea leaves will be naturally fermented. When the leaves turn three parts red and seven parts black, it is the best time for roasting to achieve the most delicious aroma.
