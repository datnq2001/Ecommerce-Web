ROBUSTA COFFEE

Robusta coffee, also known as Vietnamese Coffee, is a common variety grown in Vietnam, especially in the Central Highlands. Vietnam is currently the world's leading producer and exporter of Robusta coffee. Robusta coffee beans are round, dark-colored, with a caffeine content of about 2 - 2.5%.

CULI COFFEE

Culinary Coffee, also known as Peaberry, has a strong bitter taste and a captivating aroma. Culinary coffee beans are unique beans with only one seed from common varieties such as Arabica or Robusta, accounting for about 5% of the total coffee quantity in a harvest, always carefully selected and roasted separately to ensure perfect quality.

KING COFFEE

King Coffee is a harmonious blend of three types of beans: the deep bitterness of Moka beans, the bold bitterness of Robusta beans, and the intense bitterness of Culi beans, creating a strong flavor profile.
